Our Vision

A future where every refugee youth is digitally skilled, emotionally strong, and economically empowered to lead change in their community.

Our Mission

To equip refugee youth with digital skills, mental health support, and career pathways that lead to dignified jobs, entrepreneurship and lasting self-reliance.

Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Accountability, Inclusivity, Refugee Leadership, Gender Equality, Youth Empowerment, Mental Well-being

About REACH

Refugee Employment Accelerator and Career Hub

A future where every refugee youth is digitally skilled, emotionally strong, and economically empowered to lead change in their community.

REACH Vision

REACH is a refugee-led, non-profit social venture founded in 2025, dedicated to equipping refugee youth with digital skills, mental health support, and career pathways that lead to dignified jobs, entrepreneurship and lasting self-reliance.
